About PageLoop

PageLoop is a specialized peer-to-peer platform designed specifically for students and educators to safely exchange academic materials, reference textbooks, and curriculum notes.

For Students & Buyers

Browse through lists of verified study resources uploaded by peers within your academic community. Connect directly, negotiate fair pricing via our system, and save on costly educational texts.

For Material Sellers

Turn your old textbooks and comprehensive course study logs into extra income. To protect the integrity of the platform and prevent malicious spam listings, we verify all seller profiles securely.

Note for Sellers: A secure, one-time activation platform fee of ₹30 applies upon setting up your storefront to verify your ledger account permanently.
